GEOG 572 - Lab 1 Scrollytelling

My visual story explores the prevalence of romance scams across the United States, highlighting how thousands of people are targeted online every year, particularly through dating platforms. I chose this topic because online dating is such a prominent part of modern dating culture, and I wanted to examine not just the risks, but also the real impact of these scams on individuals. My background in computer science and interest in cybersecurity made this topic particularly compelling, as it allowed me to combine my technical curiosity with a timely social issue. My primary audience was anyone who engages in dating culture, whether that means they partake in dating themselves or they see their friends and family participating, because awareness of how romance scams operate is crucial for staying safe online.
