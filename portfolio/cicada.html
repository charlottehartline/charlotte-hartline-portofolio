Brood XIII Cicada Emergence (2024)

GEOG 370 - Final Project

This map is both a geographic study and a personal reflection on the Brood XIII cicada emergence in the Midwest, focusing on Illinois, Wisconsin, Iowa, and Michigan. Using iNaturalist data and historical brood ranges, the map reveals not just where the cicadas appeared most densely, but where memories, sounds, and sensations are tied and the excitement of witnessing a rare natural event after so many years.
